Meet Our Robots

Suvai – Dining Service Robot

Dining & Delivery Service

Suvai is an intelligent dining robot designed to enhance guest experiences at events by delivering refreshments, sweets, flowers, or whatever the need may be. With its sleek tray system and interactive voice prompts, Suvai brings a blend of efficiency, technology, and hospitality to modern event settings.

  • Smart Serving System
  • Interactive Voice Commands
  • Elevated Guest Experience

Smiley – Selfie Robot

Interactive Photo & Print

Smiley is an interactive selfie robot designed to capture memorable moments at events. It moves through the venue clicking photos of guests and instantly printing them, while also offering easy digital access through a scan of a QR code.

  • Instant Photo Printing
  • Easy Digital Access
  • Static or Roaming Mode

Robo Dog – Entertainment Robot

Interactive & Fun Experience

Robo Dog is an interactive robotic companion designed purely for entertainment at events. With its playful movements and expressive gestures, it delights both kids and adults, adding a fun and engaging element to the overall event experience.

  • Interactive Gestures
  • Crowd Engagement
  • Great Photo Opportunity
